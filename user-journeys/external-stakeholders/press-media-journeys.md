# External Stakeholders: Press & Media Journeys

## Overview

This document outlines the key user journeys for press and media professionals who cover PenguinMails as part of their technology journalism, business coverage, or industry analysis. These journeys focus on story development, research processes, interview coordination, and content creation.

**Purpose**: Provide clear workflows for journalists and media professionals to access relevant information and create compelling, accurate content about PenguinMails.

---

## User Profile: Press & Media

### **Media Categories**

#### **Technology Journalists**
- Focus on product features, technology innovation, and platform capabilities
- Cover industry trends and competitive positioning
- Evaluate technical differentiation and competitive advantages
- Assess market opportunity and growth potential

#### **Business Press**
- Focus on company stories, growth narratives, and market positioning
- Cover funding rounds, leadership changes, and strategic developments
- Analyze business model, revenue growth, and market expansion
- Evaluate management team and organizational capability

#### **Trade Publications**
- Industry-specific coverage for marketing technology and SaaS markets
- Focus on customer use cases, ROI analysis, and industry adoption
- Cover regulatory changes, compliance, and market standards
- Assess industry trends and future outlook

#### **Podcast Hosts & Content Creators**
- Interview-based content creation and discussion
- Focus on founder stories, industry insights, and thought leadership
- Cover company milestones, product launches, and strategic developments
- Analyze market trends and future predictions

### **Common Information Needs**
- Company background and founder stories
- Product differentiation and competitive advantages
- Customer success stories and use cases
- Market analysis and industry trends
- Technology innovation and IP developments
- Financial performance and growth metrics

## Core Press & Media Journeys

### **Journey 1: Story Discovery & Research**

#### **Topic Identification**
1. **News Monitoring**
   - Monitor PenguinMails website for announcements and updates
   - Track social media presence and public communications
   - Review industry publications for relevant coverage opportunities
   - Identify trending topics and industry developments

2. **Story Angle Development**
   - Research company background and founding story
   - Analyze product features and competitive positioning
   - Investigate customer success stories and case studies
   - Assess market trends and industry dynamics

#### **Initial Research Process**
1. **Background Research**
   - Review company website, about pages, and product information
   - Analyze press releases and public announcements
   - Research founding team background and experience
   - Review previous media coverage and interviews

2. **Market Context Analysis**
   - Research email deliverability and marketing automation market
   - Analyze competitive landscape and positioning
   - Review industry reports and analyst coverage
   - Assess market trends and growth opportunities

#### **Story Planning**
1. **Angle & Focus Development**
   - Identify unique story angles and perspectives
   - Plan interview questions and discussion topics
   - Determine key messages and narrative arc
   - Consider audience and publication requirements

2. **Resource Planning**
   - Identify needed information and sources
   - Plan interview scheduling and coordination
   - Determine demonstration or product review needs
   - Schedule follow-up research and verification

---

### **Journey 2: Information Gathering & Verification**

#### **Primary Source Interviews**
1. **Founder & Leadership Interviews**
   - Schedule interviews with CEO, CTO, and key executives
   - Prepare thoughtful, well-researched questions
   - Focus on company vision, strategy, and market positioning
   - Discuss technology innovation and competitive advantages

2. **Customer & User Interviews**
   - Identify and contact customer references
   - Conduct interviews with end users and administrators
   - Gather use cases, success stories, and testimonials
   - Verify product capabilities and real-world impact

#### **Technical & Product Research**
1. **Product Demonstration**
   - Request platform demonstrations and walkthroughs
   - Evaluate product features and capabilities
   - Assess user experience and interface design
   - Test integration capabilities and platform strength

2. **Competitive Analysis**
   - Research direct and indirect competitors
   - Analyze feature comparisons and differentiation
   - Review pricing strategies and positioning
   - Assess competitive advantages and market dynamics

#### **Market & Industry Analysis**
1. **Market Research**
   - Analyze market size, growth, and opportunity
   - Research industry trends and future outlook
   - Review regulatory changes and compliance requirements
   - Assess customer adoption patterns and behavior

2. **Financial & Business Research**
   - Review funding history and investor information
   - Analyze business model and revenue streams
   - Research customer acquisition and retention metrics
   - Assess growth trajectory and scaling capability

---

### **Journey 3: Content Creation & Production**

#### **Article Development**
1. **Writing Process**
   - Structure article with compelling headline and lead
   - Organize content with clear, logical flow
   - Include relevant quotes and expert opinions
   - Support claims with data and evidence

2. **Fact-Checking & Verification**
   - Verify all factual claims and statistics
   - Confirm quotes and attributions
   - Review technical accuracy and product details
   - Ensure compliance with publication standards

#### **Multimedia Content Creation**
1. **Visual Content**
   - Source or create relevant images and graphics
   - Capture screenshots of product interfaces
   - Develop infographics and data visualizations
   - Ensure proper licensing and attribution

2. **Audio & Video Content**
   - Conduct recorded interviews and podcasts
   - Create product demonstration videos
   - Develop social media content and clips
   - Ensure audio/video quality and professionalism

#### **Publication Preparation**
1. **Editorial Review**
   - Submit content for editorial review and approval
   - Address editorial feedback and revisions
   - Ensure compliance with publication guidelines
   - Finalize content for publication

2. **Promotion Planning**
   - Plan social media promotion and engagement
   - Coordinate with PR team for amplification
   - Prepare follow-up content and responses
   - Monitor audience engagement and feedback

---

### **Journey 4: Ongoing Coverage & Relationship Building**

#### **Relationship Management**
1. **PR Team Coordination**
   - Maintain regular communication with PR representatives
   - Request briefings and background information
   - Coordinate interview scheduling and logistics
   - Ensure consistent messaging and information

2. **Source Relationship Building**
   - Develop relationships with key company executives
   - Maintain contact with customer references and users
   - Build network of industry experts and analysts
   - Foster ongoing dialogue and information sharing

#### **Industry Coverage**
1. **Trend Analysis**
   - Monitor industry developments and trends
   - Analyze competitive moves and market changes
   - Track regulatory developments and compliance issues
   - Assess technology innovation and disruption

2. **Thought Leadership**
   - Participate in industry conferences and events
   - Contribute to industry publications and blogs
   - Engage with industry thought leaders and experts
   - Share insights and analysis on market developments

## Press & Media Context

### **Key Pain Points**
- Difficulty accessing real customers for verification and stories
- Limited access to technical details and competitive information
- Time pressure to produce accurate, well-researched content
- Balancing editorial independence with source relationships
- Keeping pace with rapidly evolving technology landscape

### **Success Metrics**
- **Content Quality**: Accurate, well-researched, and compelling coverage
- **Source Relationships**: Strong, trust-based relationships with company sources
- **Industry Impact**: Influential coverage that shapes market perception
- **Audience Engagement**: High engagement and readership metrics
- **Professional Recognition**: Recognition as authoritative industry coverage

## Common Press & Media Workflows

### **Story Development Cycle**
1. **Week 1: Research & Planning**
   - Initial research and story angle development
   - Source identification and preliminary interviews
   - Background research and fact-gathering
   - Story outline and structure planning

2. **Week 2-3: Information Gathering**
   - Conduct primary source interviews
   - Gather supporting data and evidence
   - Verify facts and claims
   - Complete product demonstrations and technical research

3. **Week 4: Content Creation**
   - Write and structure article content
   - Develop multimedia elements and visuals
   - Conduct fact-checking and verification
   - Prepare for publication and promotion

### **Ongoing Coverage**
1. **Daily Monitoring**
   - Monitor company communications and announcements
   - Track social media and industry developments
   - Identify story opportunities and angles
   - Maintain source relationships and communication

2. **Weekly Planning**
   - Plan coverage priorities and story development
   - Coordinate interviews and information gathering
   - Review editorial calendar and publication deadlines
   - Assess competitor coverage and market dynamics

3. **Monthly Reviews**
   - Review coverage performance and audience engagement
   - Assess source relationships and information flow
   - Plan strategic coverage and relationship building
   - Evaluate industry trends and market developments

## Best Practices & Standards

### **Editorial Standards**
- **Accuracy**: Verify all facts and claims before publication
- **Objectivity**: Maintain editorial independence and balanced perspective
- **Transparency**: Disclose sources and potential conflicts of interest
- **Fairness**: Provide opportunity for response and clarification

### **Source Management**
- **Professional Relationships**: Maintain professional, respectful interactions
- **Information Integrity**: Ensure accurate information sharing and attribution
- **Confidentiality**: Respect confidential information and embargo agreements
- **Long-term Engagement**: Foster ongoing relationships for future coverage

### **Content Quality**
- **Research Depth**: Conduct thorough research and fact-gathering
- **Storytelling**: Create compelling, well-structured narratives
- **Audience Focus**: Tailor content to target audience needs and interests
- **Industry Impact**: Contribute meaningful insights to industry discourse
