# Template Selection Guide

**Quick Access**: Learn how to choose the perfect email templates from our library based on campaign goals and performance metrics.

## Overview

Selecting the right template is crucial for campaign success. This guide covers systematic approaches to choose templates based on campaign type, industry requirements, and performance indicators.

---

## Choose Templates by Purpose

### Campaign Type Selection

### Welcome Series Templates

- **Clean & Simple Welcome**: For minimal brands, SaaS products
- **Feature Showcase**: For complex products with multiple key features
- **Onboarding Checklist**: For step-by-step user journeys
- **Video Welcome**: For high-touch, personal brands

### Newsletter Templates

- **Single Article Focus**: Blog updates, company news
- **Multi-Story Digest**: Weekly roundups, industry updates
- **Curated Links**: Resource collections, curated content
- **Image-Heavy**: Visual content, photography, portfolios

### Promotional Templates

- **Product Launch**: New feature announcements
- **Sale/Promotion**: Discount codes, limited-time offers
- **Event Invitation**: Webinars, conferences, meetups
- **Social Proof**: Testimonials, case studies

### Industry-Specific Selection

### SaaS & Technology

- Clean, modern designs with technical emphasis
- Feature-focused layouts
- Simple navigation structures

### E-commerce

- Product showcase emphasis
- Shopping cart integration
- Mobile-optimized for impulse purchasing

### Professional Services

- Conservative, trustworthy designs
- Case study and testimonial integration
- Contact-focused layouts

### Healthcare

- Clean, trustworthy designs
- Privacy-conscious layouts
- Compliance-friendly structure

## Template Quality Assessment

### Performance Indicators

### High-Performing Templates (Choose These First)

- Average open rate above 25%
- Average click rate above 3%
- Recent usage (last 30 days)
- High user ratings (4.0+ stars)

### Quality Metrics to Review

```text
Performance Rating: ★★★★☆ (4.2/5.0)
Usage Count: 1,247 campaigns
Avg Open Rate: 42% (Industry: 35%)
Avg Click Rate: 12% (Industry: 8%)
Last Used: 2 days ago
Compatibility: Mobile, Desktop, All Email Clients
```

### Template Components Check

Before selecting a template, ensure it includes:

- **Header with Logo**: Branded header section
- **Hero Section**: Main visual focus area
- **Content Blocks**: Flexible content sections
- **Call-to-Action**: Clear action buttons
- **Footer**: Contact info and social links
- **Unsubscribe**: Compliance elements

## Selection Process

### Step 1: Define Campaign Goals

### Primary Objectives

- Brand awareness
- Lead generation
- Customer engagement
- Sales conversion
- Customer retention

### Step 2: Match Template to Goals

### Awareness Campaigns

- Newsletter templates with broad appeal
- Educational content layouts
- Social sharing optimization

### Conversion Campaigns

- Product showcase templates
- Clear CTA-focused designs
- Minimal distractions

### Retention Campaigns

- Personal, relationship-focused designs
- Customer success stories
- Exclusive content emphasis

### Step 3: Test Template Compatibility

### Technical Checklist

- [ ] Mobile responsive design
- [ ] Cross-email client compatibility
- [ ] Image optimization
- [ ] Load time performance
