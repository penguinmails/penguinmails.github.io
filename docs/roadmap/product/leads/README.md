# Lead Management Roadmap

## Overview

This roadmap outlines the development timeline for lead management features, including MVP priorities and Post-MVP enhancements.

**MVP Timeline:** Q1 2026
**Post-MVP Timeline:** Q1 2026 - Q4 2027

## MVP Roadmap Items (Q1 2026)

### Critical Blockers (P0)

* **[Contact Import/Export System](contact-import-export.md)** (P0): CSV/Excel import with field mapping.
* **[Basic Contact Segmentation](basic-segmentation.md)** (P0): Static lists for campaign targeting.

## Post-MVP Roadmap (Q1 2026 - Q4 2027)

### Q1 2026: Core Enhancements

* **[Dynamic Contact Segmentation](dynamic-segmentation.md)** (P1): Rule-based filtering and auto-updates.
* **[Basic Lead Scoring System](basic-lead-scoring.md)** (P1): Behavioral scoring based on engagement.
* **[Contact Activity Timeline](contact-activity-timeline.md)** (P1): Chronological history of interactions.
* **[Bulk Contact Operations](bulk-contact-operations.md)** (P2): Bulk actions for efficiency.
* **[Lead Enrichment](lead-enrichment.md)** (P2): Automatic data enhancement.

### Q2 2026: Advanced Features

* **[Advanced Lead Scoring Models](advanced-lead-scoring.md)** (P2): Custom formulas and multiple models.
* **[Contact Deduplication & Merge](contact-deduplication.md)** (P2): Fuzzy matching and manual merge.

### Q3 2026: CRM-Like Features

* **[Contact Lifecycle Stages](contact-lifecycle-stages.md)** (P3): Funnel tracking and transitions.
* **[Contact Notes & Tasks](contact-notes-tasks.md)** (P3): Sales collaboration tools.

### Research Spikes

* **[Third-Party Integrations Research](third-party-integrations-spike.md)** (P3): Evaluate ROI of integrations.
* **[Lead Analytics Improvements](lead-analytics-spike.md)** (P3): Explore advanced analytics capabilities.
