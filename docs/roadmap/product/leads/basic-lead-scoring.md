# Basic Lead Scoring System

## Overview

**Priority**: P1 (Post-MVP)
**Timeline**: Q1 2026 (1-2 weeks)
**Effort**: Small

**Description**:
Simple behavioral lead scoring based on email engagement (opens, clicks, replies) with recency weighting.

## Capabilities

- Engagement score calculation
- Recency weighting
- Score display
- Score-based segmentation
- Manual override
- Real-time recalculation

## Dependencies

- Contact database
- Email tracking
