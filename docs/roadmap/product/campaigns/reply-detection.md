# Reply Detection and Management

## Overview

**Priority**: P1 (High Value)
**Timeline**: Q1 2026 (5-7 days)
**Effort**: Small

**Description**:
IMAP integration for detecting replies, parsing threads, and attributing replies to specific campaigns.

## Capabilities

- IMAP integration
- Reply parsing and threading
- Automatic routing to unified inbox
- Reply notification
- Reply rate tracking
- Reply attribution

## Dependencies

- Unified inbox (Blocked by Stalwart investigation)
- IMAP integration
- Campaign management system
