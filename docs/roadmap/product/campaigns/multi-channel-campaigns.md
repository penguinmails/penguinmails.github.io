# Multi-Channel Campaigns

## Overview

**Priority**: P3 (Post-MVP)
**Effort**: Large (6 weeks)
**Timeline**: Q1 2027

**Description**:
Unified campaigns that can send across Email, SMS, and Push notifications.

**Acceptance Criteria**:

- Multi-channel campaign wizard
- Channel-specific content editors
- Audience segmentation by channel preference
- Unified reporting (Total reach, engagement)
- Cost estimation per channel

**Dependencies**:

- Campaign Management MVP
- SMS/Push infrastructure
