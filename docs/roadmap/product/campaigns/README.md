# Campaign Management Roadmap

## Overview

This roadmap outlines the development timeline for campaign management features, from MVP launch through advanced AI-powered capabilities.

**MVP Launch:** Q1 2026
**Post-MVP Enhancements:** Q2 2026 - Q4 2026

## MVP Roadmap Items (Q1 2026)

### Critical Launch Blockers (P0)

* **[Bounce Handling System](bounce-handling.md)** (P0): Automated bounce detection and suppression.
* **[Unsubscribe Management System](unsubscribe-management.md)** (P0): Legal requirement for opt-out handling.
* **[Spam Complaint Handling](spam-complaint-handling.md)** (P0): Feedback loop integration and suppression.
* **[Campaign Scheduling (Basic)](campaign-scheduling.md)** (P0): Immediate and scheduled sending.
* **[Basic Campaign Analytics](basic-campaign-analytics.md)** (P0): Real-time metrics and reporting.

### High-Value Features (P1)

* **[A/B Testing (Basic)](ab-testing-basic.md)** (P1): Subject line testing and winner selection.
* **[Reply Detection and Management](reply-detection.md)** (P1): IMAP integration for reply tracking.

## Post-MVP Roadmap (Q2 2026 - Q4 2026)

### Q2 2026: Testing & Optimization

* **[Advanced Multi-Variate Testing](multivariate-testing.md)** (P2): Comprehensive testing framework.

### Q3 2026: AI-Powered Intelligence

* **[Advanced Personalization Engine](advanced-personalization.md)** (P2): Predictive content and dynamic generation.
* **[Smart Campaign Sequencing](smart-sequencing.md)** (P2): Adaptive timing and engagement-based branching.
* **[Advanced Template Intelligence](template-intelligence.md)** (P2): Smart recommendations and optimization.

### Q4 2026: Enterprise & Advanced

* **[AI-Powered Campaign Optimization](ai-campaign-optimization.md)** (P2): Auto-optimization of campaign parameters.
* **[Predictive Sending Intelligence](predictive-sending.md)** (P2): Performance forecasting and risk assessment.
* **[Intelligent Deliverability Management](intelligent-deliverability.md)** (P2): Proactive reputation management.
* **[Campaign Workflow Automation](campaign-workflow-automation.md)** (P2): Complex multi-channel workflows.
* **[Advanced Analytics and Attribution](advanced-analytics-attribution.md)** (P2): Multi-touch attribution and ROI tracking.
