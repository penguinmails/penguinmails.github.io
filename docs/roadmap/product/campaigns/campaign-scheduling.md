# Campaign Scheduling (Basic)

## Overview

**Priority**: P0 (Launch Blocker)
**Timeline**: Q1 2026 (3-4 days)
**Effort**: Small

**Description**:
Core functionality allowing users to schedule campaigns for immediate or future sending, including timezone support.

## Capabilities

- Immediate send
- Scheduled send
- Timezone-aware delivery
- Schedule editing
- Schedule cancellation

## Dependencies

- Campaign management system
- Background job scheduler
