# Advanced Campaign Analytics

## Overview

**Priority**: P2 (Post-MVP)
**Effort**: Medium (3-4 weeks)
**Timeline**: Q3 2026

**Description**:
Deep dive analytics for campaigns including heatmaps, geo-tracking, and device stats.

**Acceptance Criteria**:

- Click heatmaps (visual overlay on email)
- Geo-map of opens and clicks
- Device and client breakdown (Mobile vs. Desktop, Gmail vs. Outlook)
- Read time analysis (Skimmed vs. Read)
- Conversion tracking (Revenue attribution)
- Exportable PDF reports

**Dependencies**:

- Analytics infrastructure (ClickHouse)
