# Dedicated Support & SLA

## Overview

**Priority**: P3 (Enterprise)
**Effort**: Small (Process/Ops)
**Timeline**: Q3 2027

**Description**:
Premium support tiers with dedicated resources and guaranteed response times.

**Features**:

- **Priority Support Queue**: Enterprise tickets bypass standard queue.
- **Dedicated Account Manager**: Named contact for enterprise accounts.
- **Phone Support**: 24/7 emergency phone line.
- **Quarterly Business Reviews**: Strategic planning sessions.
- **Custom Training**: Onboarding and training sessions.

**Dependencies**:

- Support ticketing system integration
- Staffing
