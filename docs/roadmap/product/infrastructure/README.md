# Infrastructure Management Roadmap

## Overview

This roadmap outlines the development timeline for infrastructure management features, including backup/recovery, scaling, monitoring, and multi-region deployment.

**MVP Completion:** Q1 2026
**Post-MVP Timeline:** Q2 2026 - Q1 2027

## MVP Roadmap Items (Q1 2026)

### Critical Blockers (P0)

* **[Infrastructure Backup & Recovery System](backup-recovery.md)** (P0): Automated backups and point-in-time recovery.

### High Priority (P1)

* **[Infrastructure Scaling UI with Resource Limits](scaling-ui.md)** (P1): Real-time resource usage and one-click scaling.
* **[IP Reputation Management User Controls](ip-reputation-controls.md)** (P1): Dashboard for IP reputation and blacklist monitoring.
* **[Infrastructure Cost Tracking & Billing](cost-tracking.md)** (P1): Real-time cost tracking and allocation.

### Medium Priority (P2)

* **[DNS Propagation Status & Troubleshooting](dns-propagation.md)** (P2): Real-time DNS checker and troubleshooting wizard.
* **[Infrastructure Health Alerts & Notifications](health-alerts.md)** (P2): Multi-channel alerting for infrastructure issues.
* **[VPS Resource Usage History & Trends](resource-usage-history.md)** (P2): Historical usage data and capacity planning.

## Post-MVP Roadmap (Q2 2026 - Q1 2027)

### Q2 2026: Multi-Region

* **[Multi-Region Infrastructure Deployment](multi-region-deployment.md)** (P1): Global deployment with intelligent routing.

### Q3 2026: Auto-Scaling

* **[Auto-Scaling Infrastructure Based on Load](auto-scaling.md)** (P1): Automated vertical and horizontal scaling.

### Q4 2026: Disaster Recovery

* **[Disaster Recovery & Business Continuity](disaster-recovery.md)** (P0): Automated failover and geo-redundant backups.

### Q1 2027: Provider Flexibility

* **[Alternative VPS Providers](alternative-providers.md)** (P2): Support for AWS, DigitalOcean, and others.
