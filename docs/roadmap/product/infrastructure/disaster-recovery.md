# Disaster Recovery & Business Continuity

## Overview

**Priority**: P0 (Post-MVP)
**Timeline**: Q4 2026
**Effort**: Large

**Description**:
Automated failover to backup regions and geo-redundant backups to ensure business continuity.

## Capabilities

- Automated failover (RTO < 15 mins)
- Geo-redundant backup storage
- Hot standby infrastructure
- Automated DR testing
- Runbook automation

## Dependencies

- Multi-region infrastructure
- Geo-redundant backups
