# Engagement-Based Routing

## Overview

**Priority**: Medium
**Timeline**: Q4 2026
**Effort**: Part of Intelligent Deliverability (7-9 weeks total)

**Description**:
Route emails through best IPs based on recipient engagement history to maximize inbox placement for high-value contacts.

## Capabilities

- Identify high-engagement recipients
- Route high-value emails through premium IPs
- Isolate low-engagement traffic

## Dependencies

- Multiple IP infrastructure
- Engagement tracking
