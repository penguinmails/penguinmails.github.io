# Deliverability Scoring

## Overview

**Priority**: Medium
**Timeline**: Q4 2026
**Effort**: Part of Intelligent Deliverability (7-9 weeks total)

**Description**:
Score campaigns for deliverability risk before sending to help users improve their content and targeting.

## Capabilities

- Analyze content for spam triggers
- Evaluate list quality and engagement history
- Provide a 0-100 deliverability score
- Suggest improvements to increase score

## Dependencies

- ML infrastructure
- Content analysis tools
