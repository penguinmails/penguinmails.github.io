# Team & Workspace Management Roadmap

## Overview

This roadmap outlines the development timeline for team and workspace management features, from MVP completion through enterprise-grade collaboration capabilities.

**Current Status:** Partially Complete (Core features functional, 7 MVP gaps)

## MVP Roadmap Items (Q1 2026)

### High Priority

* **[Workspace Management Feature Documentation](workspace-management-docs.md)** (P1): Comprehensive documentation for workspace creation and management.
* **[Workspace Health Scoring System](workspace-health-scoring.md)** (P1): Algorithm and UI for workspace health scores.
* **[Organization Settings & Branding Documentation](organization-settings-docs.md)** (P1): Documentation for organization-level settings and branding.
* **[RBAC Permission Matrix Documentation](rbac-permission-matrix-docs.md)** (P1): Detailed permission matrix for all roles.
* **[Team Member Removal Workflow Documentation](team-member-removal-docs.md)** (P1): Workflow for removing team members and handling data.
* **[Workspace Member Management Documentation](workspace-member-management-docs.md)** (P1): Documentation for workspace-specific member management.
* **[Workspace Deletion & Data Handling Documentation](workspace-deletion-docs.md)** (P1): Process for workspace deletion and data retention.

## Post-MVP Roadmap (Q2 2026 - Q3 2027)

### Q2 2026: Advanced Permissions

* **[Advanced Permissions System (Custom Roles)](custom-roles.md)** (P2): Custom role creation with granular permissions.

### Q3 2026: Audit Logs & Bulk Management

* **[Audit Logs for Team Actions](audit-logs.md)** (P2): Comprehensive audit trail of team management actions.
* **[Bulk User Management](bulk-user-management.md)** (P3): Bulk invitation and management of team members.

### Q4 2026: Analytics & Security

* **[Team Analytics & Activity Monitoring](team-analytics.md)** (P3): Dashboard for team member activity and productivity.
* **[Advanced Session Management](advanced-session-management.md)** (P2): Enhanced session tracking and security.

### Q1 2027: User Groups & Onboarding

* **[User Groups & Teams Within Tenant](user-groups.md)** (P3): Logical groups of users for easier management.
* **[Spike: Granular Role Granularity](role-granularity-spike.md)** (P3): Evaluate need for more granular roles.
* **[Team Member Onboarding Workflows](onboarding-workflows.md)** (P3): Automated onboarding for new team members.

### Q2 2027: Templates & Client Portal

* **[Workspace Templates](workspace-templates.md)** (P3): Pre-configured workspace templates.
* **[External User Collaboration (Client Portal)](client-portal.md)** (P3): Limited access for external clients.

### Q3 2027: Advanced Features

* **[Workspace Duplication](workspace-duplication.md)** (P3): Functionality to clone existing workspaces.
