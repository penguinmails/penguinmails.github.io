# Billing & Subscriptions MVP Completion

**Status**: MVP
**Timeline**: Q2 2026
**Feature Specification**: [Payment Features](/docs/features/payments/README)

## Details

- **Plan Upgrade/Downgrade UI**: Visual plan comparison, proration preview
- **Usage Tracking Dashboard**: Real-time progress bars, alerts
- **Invoice Management System**: Direct PDF download, invoice preview
- **Payment Method Management**: In-app card updates via Stripe Elements
- **Failed Payment Recovery**: Dashboard banner, one-click retry
- **Subscription Cancellation Flow**: Cancellation survey, retention offers
- **Budget Controls**: Budget limits, alert thresholds

### Business Impact

- **Revenue Protection**: 40% reduction in payment failure churn
- **Self-Service**: 80%+ plan changes without support intervention
- **Support Efficiency**: 30% reduction in billing support tickets
- **Customer Satisfaction**: Professional invoice management
