### Client Assessment Framework

#### Discovery Question Set

**Business and Goals:**

1. What are your primary business objectives for email marketing?
2. Who is your target audience and customer personas?
3. What is your current email marketing maturity level?
4. What challenges are you facing with your current email marketing?
5. What timeline and budget do you have for email marketing initiatives?

**Technical and Resources:**

1. What email marketing platform are you currently using?
2. What is your current subscriber list size and quality?
3. What content creation resources do you have available?
4. What integration needs do you have with other marketing tools?
5. What reporting and analytics requirements do you have?

**Strategy and Expectations:**

1. What success metrics and KPIs are most important to you?
2. What is your preferred communication style and frequency?
3. What are your expectations for timeline and deliverables?
4. What additional services or support do you anticipate needing?
5. What concerns or constraints should we be aware of?
