## Customer Health Monitoring Templates

### Monthly Health Score Calculation Template

**Health Score Components (Weighted)**:
- Usage Frequency (30%): Login patterns, feature adoption, engagement levels
- Feature Adoption (25%): Core feature usage, advanced feature exploration
- Support Interaction (20%): Ticket volume, resolution time, satisfaction scores
- Payment Status (15%): On-time payments, billing issues, account standing
- Engagement Level (10%): Communication responsiveness, training participation

**Health Score Ranges**:
- **90-100**: Healthy - High engagement, expansion opportunity
- **70-89**: Stable - Regular usage, monitor for growth
- **50-69**: At-Risk - Declining usage, proactive outreach needed
- **Below 50**: Critical - Immediate intervention required

**Monthly Review Process**:
1. Calculate health scores using weighted components
2. Identify score changes >15 points from previous month
3. Flag accounts in At-Risk or Critical ranges
4. Schedule proactive outreach for declining accounts
5. Document intervention actions and outcomes

## Retention Strategy Templates

### Customer Lifecycle Stage Definitions

**Stage 1: Onboarding (Days 1-30)**
- Goal: Successful platform adoption and first-value achievement
- Key Metrics: Time-to-first-value, feature activation rate, onboarding completion
- Success Criteria: 80% feature activation, 3+ active users, first campaign sent

**Stage 2: Active Growth (Months 2-6)**
- Goal: Expand usage and demonstrate ongoing value
- Key Metrics: Feature adoption growth, user expansion, campaign frequency
- Success Criteria: 5+ active features, user growth >20%, weekly campaign activity

**Stage 3: Mature & Expansion (Months 7+)**
- Goal: Maximize platform value and identify expansion opportunities
- Key Metrics: Advanced feature usage, account expansion, referral potential
- Success Criteria: 8+ active features, multi-team usage, upgrade consideration

**Stage 4: At-Risk/Renewal (30-60 days before renewal)**
- Goal: Ensure renewal and address concerns
- Key Metrics: Usage trends, satisfaction scores, engagement levels
- Success Criteria: Stable/growing usage, resolved concerns, renewal commitment

## Expansion Opportunity Templates

### Upgrade Qualification Framework

**Plan Upgrade Indicators**:
- Email volume exceeding current plan limits by 20%+
- Feature requests for premium-tier capabilities
- Multi-team usage requiring additional seats
- Advanced automation needs beyond current plan

**Upgrade Conversation Template**:
1. **Data Review**: Present usage trends showing plan limit approaches
2. **Value Demonstration**: Show ROI of current usage and projected expansion value
3. **Feature Alignment**: Highlight premium features matching their use cases
4. **Pricing Clarity**: Provide transparent upgrade costs with ROI justification
5. **Timeline**: Establish decision timeline and implementation plan

**Cross-Sell Opportunity Indicators**:
- High engagement in specific feature categories
- Manual processes that could be automated with add-ons
- Integration requests for complementary services
- Team expansion into new use cases

## Churn Prevention Templates

### Early Warning Signal Checklist

**Usage Decline Signals**:
- [ ] Login frequency decreased >30% month-over-month
- [ ] Feature usage dropped to <3 active features
- [ ] Campaign send volume decreased >40%
- [ ] No activity for 14+ consecutive days

**Engagement Decline Signals**:
- [ ] Email open rates for customer communications <20%
- [ ] Declined training invitations or webinar participation
- [ ] Unresponsive to customer success outreach (3+ attempts)
- [ ] Reduced support ticket submissions (may indicate disengagement)

**Payment & Administrative Signals**:
- [ ] Late payments or billing disputes
- [ ] Downgrade requests or pricing objections
- [ ] Key stakeholder turnover without replacement contact
- [ ] Contract renewal date approaching (<60 days)

**Intervention Protocol**:
1. **Immediate Response** (Within 24 hours): Contact assigned CSM for account review
2. **Root Cause Analysis** (Within 48 hours): Identify specific decline triggers
3. **Action Plan Development** (Within 72 hours): Create targeted intervention strategy
4. **Executive Escalation** (If needed): Engage leadership for high-value accounts
5. **Progress Monitoring** (Weekly): Track intervention effectiveness and adjust approach

## Win-Back Campaign Templates

### Churned Customer Re-Engagement Framework

**Segmentation Criteria**:
- **Recent Churn** (0-3 months): High re-engagement potential
- **Mid-Term Churn** (3-6 months): Moderate potential, needs strong value proposition
- **Long-Term Churn** (6+ months): Lower potential, focus on major improvements

**Re-Engagement Campaign Structure**:
1. **Initial Outreach** (Week 1): Personalized message acknowledging past relationship
2. **Value Demonstration** (Week 2): Showcase new features and improvements since departure
3. **Exclusive Offer** (Week 3): Limited-time incentive for returning customers
4. **Success Story** (Week 4): Share relevant customer success case study
5. **Final Touchpoint** (Week 5): Last opportunity with special terms

**Success Metrics**:
- Re-engagement rate: 15-20% for recent churn, 5-10% for mid-term
- Time-to-reactivation: Average 30-45 days from first contact
- Retention after return: 80%+ annual retention for re-engaged customers
