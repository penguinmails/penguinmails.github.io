## ‚úÖ The 20-Point Compliance Checklist

### 1. Legal Basis & Consent

- [ ] **Legitimate Interest Assessment (LIA)**: Have you documented *why* you have a legitimate interest in contacting this person? (e.g., B2B relevance).
- [ ] **Targeting Relevance**: Is the product/service directly relevant to the recipient's professional role?
- [ ] **No "Spray and Pray"**: Can you prove you didn't just buy a random list?
- [ ] **B2B Focus**: Are you strictly emailing business addresses (not personal @gmail.com accounts)?

### 2. Transparency & Rights

- [ ] **Identity Disclosure**: Is it clear who is sending the email? (No misleading "From" names).
- [ ] **Physical Address**: Does your email footer include a valid physical postal address?
- [ ] **Privacy Policy Link**: Is there a direct link to your privacy policy in the email footer?
- [ ] **Source of Data**: If asked, can you tell the recipient exactly where you got their data?

### 3. Opt-Out Mechanism

- [ ] **One-Click Unsubscribe**: Is there a clear, functioning unsubscribe link?
- [ ] **Immediate Processing**: Do you process unsubscribes within 30 days (ideally instantly)?
- [ ] **Suppression List**: Do you maintain a "Do Not Contact" list to prevent re-emailing unsubscribed users?
- [ ] **No "Log In to Unsubscribe"**: Is the process frictionless?

### 4. Data Management

- [ ] **Data Minimization**: Are you only storing data you actually need (Name, Email, Job Title)?
- [ ] **Data Accuracy**: Do you regularly clean your lists to remove invalid emails?
- [ ] **Data Retention Policy**: Do you delete data that is no longer needed?
- [ ] **Security**: Is your prospect data stored securely (encrypted, password protected)?

### 5. Vendor & Third-Party

- [ ] **Processor Agreements**: Do your email tools (like PenguinMails) have a Data Processing Agreement (DPA)?
- [ ] **International Transfers**: If data moves outside the EU, are Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) in place?
- [ ] **Vendor Audit**: Have you vetted your lead data providers for compliance?
- [ ] **Breach Notification**: Do you have a plan to notify authorities within 72 hours of a data breach?
