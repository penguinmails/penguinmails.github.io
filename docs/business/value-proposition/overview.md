1. **Automated Compliance Oversight**: Effortlessly meet Google & Yahoo mandates with automated DMARC, DKIM, and SPF implementation.
2. **Global Compliance Management**: Built-in automation for GDPR, CAN-SPAM, and CASL ensures you meet international regulations.
3. **Simplified for Business Users**: An intuitive interface designed for business leaders, closing the adoption gap seen with complex enterprise tools.
4. **Optimized Pricing for SMEs**: Feature-based pricing designed for the Small and Medium-sized Enterprise segment, typically in the $40-80/month range.

**Our Solution**: "Compliance-by-design eliminates regulatory risk and overhead."

**How We Solve It**:

- **Automated Compliance Checking**: Real-time validation of email content and sending practices.
- **Built-in GDPR Features**: Automate data protection, consent management, and the right to be forgotten.
- **CAN-SPAM Compliance**: Automatically include unsubscribe links and proper header information.
- **Comprehensive Audit Trails**: Maintain detailed logs for compliance documentation.

**Business Impact**:
