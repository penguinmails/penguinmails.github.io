# White Label Success Metrics

## Agency Performance

- **Client Migration Rate**: Transition 25-50% of each agency's portfolio within the first six months.
- **Retention**: Sustain >95% annual client retention for white-label accounts.
- **Revenue per Client**: Maintain $200-$300 monthly revenue with 6-10% annual growth.
- **Agency Satisfaction**: Target Net Promoter Score (NPS) above 90.
- **Platform Utilization**: Ensure at least 70% of agency team members actively use the platform.

## Platform Growth

- Acquire 10-20 new agencies per quarter once the program matures.
- Drive 100-500 new end clients per quarter via agency-led sales.
- Increase white-label ARR by 20-30% quarter-over-quarter.
- Capture 1-3% of the addressable agency market by year two.
- Reach 20% international revenue contribution by the end of year two.

## Client Outcomes

- Deliverability improvement of 3-5x across migrated domains.
- Achieve a 100% compliance audit pass rate by enforcing automated policies.
- Maintain >90% satisfaction ratings from agency client stakeholders.
- Document 10-20x ROI from campaign performance improvements.
- Keep time-to-value under 30 minutes for baseline infrastructure setup.

## Governance Cadence

- Review performance dashboards weekly with agency success managers.
- Conduct quarterly business reviews focusing on revenue expansion and compliance posture.
- Refresh playbooks after each review cycle to codify lessons learned.
