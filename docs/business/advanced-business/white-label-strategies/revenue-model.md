# White Label Revenue Model

## Pricing Structure

### Agency Platform Fees

- **Starter**: $29 per month baseline tenancy.
- **Professional**: $99 per month with advanced analytics and compliance reporting.
- **Enterprise**: $299 per month with premium support and roadmap influence.

### Revenue Sharing Mechanics

- Client subscription price range: $100-$500 per month depending on feature mix.
- Platform share: 15-25% of client subscription revenue remitted to PenguinMails.
- Agency margin: 75-85% after platform fees, enabling reinvestment in managed services.
- Example: A $100 client payment yields $75 for the agency and $25 for the platform.

### Value-Added Services

- Compliance consulting billed at $150-$300 per hour (agencies typically charge $200-$500).
- Deliverability optimization engagements span $500-$2,000.
- Custom integrations command $1,000-$5,000 per project.
- Training and certification programs priced between $500 and $1,000 per participant.

---

## Agency Financial Outlook

### Year 1 Targets

- Client base: 25-50 clients migrated into the white-label environment.
- Average revenue: $200 per client per month.
- Monthly revenue contribution: $5K-$10K.
- Annualized revenue: $60K-$120K with $45K-$90K retained margin after platform costs.

### Year 3 Vision

- Client base: 75-150 clients managed on PenguinMails infrastructure.
- Average revenue: $250 per client per month.
- Monthly revenue contribution: $18.75K-$37.5K.
- Annualized revenue: $225K-$450K plus $50K-$100K from premium services.

---

## Platform Revenue Trajectory

- Year 1 white-label share target: $100K-$200K ARR.
- Year 3 white-label share target: $2M-$5M ARR as agency network scales.
- Gross margin expectation: 85% due to shared infrastructure and automation.
- Market penetration strategy: ramp from 10-20 agencies in North America to 200-500 globally, targeting 5-10% of agencies with 10+ clients.

---

## Financial Governance

- Align sales, finance, and operations on standardized deal templates.
- Track agency-level contribution margin and upsell elasticity quarterly.
- Maintain transparent revenue-share reporting and automated payout schedules.
