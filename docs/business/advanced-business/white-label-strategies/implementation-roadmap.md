# White Label Implementation Roadmap

## Quarter 1: Foundation and Pilot

- **Month 1**: Finalize platform enhancements, run QA, and prepare enablement assets.
- **Month 2**: Onboard the first five agency partners within a controlled pilot.
- **Month 3**: Optimize migration processes and collect structured feedback.
- **Success Metrics**: Ten agencies engaged, fifty clients migrated, satisfaction above 90%.

## Quarter 2: Scale and Optimize

- **Month 4**: Expand acquisition and training programs for additional agencies.
- **Month 5**: Activate revenue-sharing payouts and related financial reporting.
- **Month 6**: Ship platform optimizations derived from pilot learnings.
- **Success Metrics**: Twenty agencies, two hundred clients, $25K MRR from white-label contracts.

## Quarter 3: Market Expansion

- **Month 7**: Enter European markets with localized compliance bundles.
- **Month 8**: Launch partner integrations with complementary CRM and marketing tools.
- **Month 9**: Close enterprise agency deals requiring custom solutions.
- **Success Metrics**: Thirty-five agencies, five hundred clients, $75K MRR.

## Quarter 4: Market Leadership

- **Month 10**: Begin APAC localization and channel recruitment.
- **Month 11**: Roll out agency certification and advanced training curriculum.
- **Month 12**: Establish marketplace and ecosystem programs for service delivery.
- **Success Metrics**: Fifty agencies, one thousand clients, $150K MRR.

## Governance

- Track roadmap milestones via quarterly release reviews.
- Align GTM, product, and operations stakeholders on cross-functional dependencies.
- Evaluate roadmap outcomes annually to refine investment priorities.
