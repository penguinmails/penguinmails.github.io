# Customer Success Framework

## Framework Overview

The Customer Success Framework provides a systematic approach to driving customer value realization, retention, and expansion through proactive health management and strategic relationship development.

## Core Framework Components

### 1. Customer Health Assessment Model

#### Health Score Calculation
```
Customer Health Score = (Usage Health × 0.35) + (Engagement Health × 0.25) + 
                       (Business Health × 0.25) + (Relationship Health × 0.15)
```

#### Health Score Categories
- **Green (80-100)**: Healthy, low-risk accounts with expansion potential
- **Yellow (60-79)**: At-risk accounts requiring attention and intervention
- **Red (0-59)**: High-risk accounts with immediate action required

#### Health Monitoring Dimensions

**Usage Health (35% weight)**
- Daily/Weekly active user metrics
- Feature adoption rates by account tier
- API usage and integration health
- Time spent in platform and session quality

**Engagement Health (25% weight)**
- Support ticket frequency and resolution time
- Training session attendance and completion
- Community participation and contribution
- Executive sponsor interaction frequency

**Business Health (25% weight)**
- Achievement of customer-defined success metrics
- ROI realization and business outcome delivery
- Strategic goal alignment and progress
- Budget utilization and value demonstration

**Relationship Health (15% weight)**
- Executive sponsor relationship strength
- Strategic alignment and partnership perception
- Stakeholder satisfaction and advocacy
- Brand perception and loyalty indicators

### 2. Customer Lifecycle Management

#### Onboarding Phase (Days 0-90)
**Objective**: Achieve time-to-value and initial success

**Key Activities**:
- Kickoff meeting with executive sponsor
- Success plan development and approval
- Technical implementation and configuration
- User training and adoption enablement
- First value milestone achievement

**Success Criteria**:
- Active user adoption within 14 days
- First business outcome achieved within 30 days
- User satisfaction score ≥4.0
- Technical implementation completed

#### Adoption Phase (Days 30-180)
**Objective**: Drive deeper platform utilization and value

**Key Activities**:
- Regular check-in meetings and progress reviews
- Advanced feature enablement and training
- Workflow optimization and best practice sharing
- ROI demonstration and value documentation
- Expansion opportunity identification

**Success Criteria**:
- 70%+ feature adoption across customer segments
- Measurable business outcome achievement
- Executive sponsor satisfaction ≥4.2
- Expansion opportunity pipeline developed

#### Expansion Phase (Days 120+)
**Objective**: Drive revenue growth through upsell and cross-sell

**Key Activities**:
- Quarterly business review (QBR) planning and execution
- ROI analysis and value demonstration
- Expansion opportunity assessment and prioritization
- Success story documentation and case study development
- Executive relationship strengthening

**Success Criteria**:
- 15-25% annual revenue expansion
- Executive sponsor advocacy and reference willingness
- Customer satisfaction score ≥4.3
- Strategic partnership development

### 3. Customer Success Playbooks

#### Green Account Playbook
**Characteristics**: High health scores, strong engagement, expansion potential

**Success Strategies**:
- **Relationship Deepening**: Regular executive touchpoints and strategic planning
- **Expansion Identification**: Systematic opportunity assessment and development
- **Reference Development**: Success story documentation and reference activities
- **Innovation Partnership**: Beta testing and feedback collaboration

**Key Metrics**:
- Health score maintenance ≥85
- Quarterly expansion conversations
- NPS score ≥9 (promoter range)
- Reference activity participation

#### Yellow Account Playbook
**Characteristics**: Declining health scores, at-risk indicators, intervention needed

**Intervention Strategies**:
- **Root Cause Analysis**: Systematic health decline investigation
- **Recovery Planning**: Action plan development with measurable milestones
- **Enhanced Support**: Increased touchpoints and proactive problem-solving
- **Value Rediscovery**: ROI reassessment and benefit communication

**Key Metrics**:
- Health score improvement trajectory
- Churn risk reduction assessment
- Support ticket trend analysis
- Customer satisfaction recovery

#### Red Account Playbook
**Characteristics**: Critical health scores, high churn risk, immediate action required

**Recovery Strategies**:
- **Emergency Response**: Executive escalation and immediate intervention
- **Root Cause Resolution**: Technical issues and business challenges addressed
- **Value Recovery**: ROI re-demonstration and benefit re-alignment
- **Strategic Review**: Executive sponsor meeting and relationship assessment

**Key Metrics**:
- Critical issue resolution timeline
- Health score recovery rate
- Executive engagement level
- Churn prevention success rate

## Implementation Methodology

### Phase 1: Assessment and Planning
1. **Customer Segmentation**: Classify accounts by value and health
2. **Health Score Implementation**: Deploy monitoring and alerting systems
3. **Team Structure**: Define success manager roles and responsibilities
4. **Technology Setup**: Configure customer success platform tools

### Phase 2: Process Implementation
1. **Playbook Deployment**: Implement account management playbooks
2. **Training and Enablement**: Success team training and certification
3. **Process Automation**: Deploy automated health monitoring and alerts
4. **Integration Setup**: Connect customer success systems with CRM and support

### Phase 3: Optimization and Scaling
1. **Performance Analysis**: Regular framework effectiveness assessment
2. **Process Refinement**: Continuous improvement based on results
3. **Advanced Analytics**: Predictive modeling and trend analysis
4. **Scaling Preparation**: Framework expansion to additional customer segments

## Success Metrics and KPIs

### Leading Indicators
- **Health Score Distribution**: Account health across customer base
- **Engagement Metrics**: Customer interaction frequency and quality
- **Adoption Rates**: Feature utilization and platform engagement
- **Support Trends**: Ticket volume and resolution metrics

### Lagging Indicators
- **Customer Retention**: Gross and net retention rates
- **Expansion Revenue**: Upsell and cross-sell success rates
- **Customer Satisfaction**: NPS scores and satisfaction surveys
- **Time to Value**: Customer success milestone achievement

### Financial Impact Metrics
- **Customer Lifetime Value**: Revenue impact of customer success interventions
- **Churn Prevention**: Revenue protection through retention efforts
- **Expansion Attribution**: Revenue growth attributable to customer success
- **ROI on Success Investment**: Return on customer success program investment
