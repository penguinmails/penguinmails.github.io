# Success Metrics Framework

## Executive Summary

**Purpose**: Establish comprehensive success metrics for analytics dashboard implementation and ongoing performance  
**Framework**: Multi-dimensional metrics across technical performance, business impact, and user adoption  
**Target Outcomes**: 25-30% improvement in retention, $2.5M annual revenue protection, 40% faster decisions  
**Measurement Timeline**: Real-time monitoring with monthly business reviews and quarterly strategic assessments
