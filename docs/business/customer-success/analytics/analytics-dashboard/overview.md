## What is the Enhanced Analytics Dashboard?

The Enhanced Analytics Dashboard transforms customer success from reactive to predictive by providing comprehensive analytics, machine learning insights, and automated reporting. This framework enables data-driven decision-making, proactive intervention strategies, and strategic planning optimization.

### Key Capabilities

- **Historical Trend Analysis**: Multi-dimensional tracking of customer health, usage patterns, and business impact
- **Predictive Analytics**: ML-based churn prediction, expansion forecasting, and health trajectory modeling
- **Advanced Reporting**: Automated executive summaries, custom dashboards, and real-time visualizations
- **Data Integration**: Multi-source data aggregation with quality validation and real-time processing
