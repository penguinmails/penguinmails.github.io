# Historical Trend Analysis Framework

## Historical Trend Analysis Framework

### 1. Multi-Dimensional Trend Tracking

#### Customer Health Score Trends

```yaml
Health Score Trend Analysis:
  Long-term Trends (12+ months):

    - Overall health trajectory patterns


    - Industry benchmark comparisons


    - Seasonal health variations


    - Intervention effectiveness trends

  Medium-term Trends (3-12 months):

    - Quarterly health score evolution


    - Account segment performance shifts


    - Success program impact analysis


    - Resource allocation effectiveness

  Short-term Trends (1-3 months):

    - Monthly health fluctuations


    - Intervention response patterns


    - Feature adoption correlations


    - Stakeholder engagement impacts

  Real-time Monitoring:

    - Daily health score changes


    - Immediate intervention triggers


    - Event-driven health impacts


    - Proactive alert generation

```

#### Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) Evolution

```yaml
CLV Trend Framework:
  Revenue Growth Tracking:

    - Historical revenue progression


    - Expansion opportunity evolution


    - Cross-sell success patterns


    - Upsell timing optimization

  Cost Analysis Trends:

    - Customer acquisition cost trends


    - Success delivery cost analysis


    - Resource utilization patterns


    - Operational efficiency metrics

  Profitability Trends:

    - Gross margin evolution


    - Net revenue retention tracking


    - Customer profitability segmentation


    - ROI measurement over time

```

```yaml
    - Regulatory filing deadlines

    - Project renewal cycles

    - Technology upgrade periods

```

### 3. Anomaly Detection Framework

#### Automated Anomaly Identification

```yaml
Anomaly Detection Rules:
  Health Score Anomalies:

    - Sudden health score drops (>20 points)


    - Unusual recovery patterns


    - Rapid health improvements


    - Consistent health plateaus

  Usage Pattern Anomalies:

    - Dramatic usage decreases


    - Unexpected usage spikes


    - New feature adoption patterns


    - User behavior shifts

  Business Impact Anomalies:

    - Revenue projection variances


    - Churn rate fluctuations


    - Expansion opportunity changes


    - Stakeholder engagement shifts

```
