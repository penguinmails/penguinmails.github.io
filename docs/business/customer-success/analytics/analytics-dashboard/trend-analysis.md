## 1. Multi-Dimensional Trend Tracking

### Customer Health Score Trends Framework

#### Long-term Health Trends (12+ Months)

**Purpose**: Strategic health assessment and long-term customer relationship evaluation  
**Business Impact**: Identify customers at risk of strategic shifts or competitive displacement  

```yaml
Long-term Trend Analysis:
  Overall Health Trajectory Patterns:
    - 12-month health score progression and trend direction
    - Seasonal variation patterns and cyclical behavior
    - Intervention impact assessment and long-term effectiveness
    - Strategic relationship health and engagement quality
  
  Industry Benchmark Comparisons:
    - Health score benchmarking against industry standards
    - Competitive positioning and market leader comparison
    - Best practice alignment and performance gaps
    - Market evolution impact on customer health
  
  Seasonal Health Variations:
    - Industry-specific seasonal patterns and cyclical impacts
    - Budget cycle impacts on customer health and engagement
    - Holiday and vacation period effects on product usage
    - Regulatory and compliance deadline impacts
  
  Intervention Effectiveness Trends:
    - Long-term intervention success rates and patterns
    - Proactive vs. reactive intervention effectiveness
    - Resource allocation optimization based on results
    - Best practice identification and scaling opportunities
```

**Key Performance Indicators**:

- **Health Score Stability**: >80% of customers maintaining stable or improving health trends
- **Intervention Success**: >75% of proactive interventions showing positive health impact
- **Benchmark Performance**: Health scores within top 25% of industry benchmarks
- **Predictive Accuracy**: >85% of health trend predictions accurate within 30-day timeframe
