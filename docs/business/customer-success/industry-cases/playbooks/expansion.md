## SaaS Playbook Template

### SaaS-Specific Framework

#### Product Adoption Optimization

```yaml
SaaS Success Framework:
  User Adoption:


    - Feature adoption tracking


    - User engagement monitoring


    - Activation milestone achievement


    - Power user identification

  Product-Led Growth:


    - Self-service onboarding optimization


    - In-product messaging effectiveness


    - Upgrade conversion tracking


    - Churn prediction and prevention

  Expansion Strategy:


    - Usage-based expansion triggers


    - Feature usage analysis


    - Seat expansion opportunities


    - Premium feature upselling


```

#### SaaS Success Metrics

```yaml
SaaS KPIs Dashboard:
  Growth Metrics:


    - Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) growth


    - Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) expansion


    - Net Revenue Retention (NRR)


    - Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) payback

  Retention Metrics:


    - Monthly Churn Rate (<5%)


    - Annual Churn Rate (<10%)


    - Logo Retention Rate (>90%)


    - Gross Revenue Retention (GRR)

  Engagement Metrics:


    - DAU)


    - Feature adoption rate (>70%)


    - Time to first value (<24 hours)


    - User satisfaction score (>4.0)


```
